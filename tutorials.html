Cajun Chicken Sausage & Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces chicken sausage, sliced
  • 1.5 pounds large shrimp, peeled and devined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons Cajun seasoning (devided)
  • 3 cloves garlic finely minced
  • 1 small yellow onion diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 green bell pepper julienned
  • 1 red bell pepper julienned
  • 1 cup silk Dairt-Free Heavy Whipped Cream (more if needed)
  • 1.5 cups low-sodium chicken broth or stock
  • 2 tablespoons salt and pepper (mixed)
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice or cauliflower rice
  • Vegan Shaved Parmesan (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh Parsley, chopped

Directions

  1. In a large bowl add shrimp, olive oil, 1/2 cajun seasoning and toss until coated
  2. In another bowl add chicken sausage other 1/2 of cajun seasoning, and a little bit of olive oil, toss until coated
  3. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook shrimp for 5 min. or until cooked through, stirring regularly. Remove from pan and set aside.
  4. In the same pan add the chicken sausage and sauté until browned. Once browned remove from pan and set aside with shrimp.
  5. In the same pan add olive oil and onion stir fry until translucent. Add the bell peppers and sauté for about 2-3 minutes. Then add garlic and cook until fragrant (about one minute).
  6. Next, pour in the vegetable broth, and allow to come to a boil and reduce down slightly (4 minutes)
  7. Reduce heat to low, add the heavy cream, and bring to a gentle simmer, while stirring occasionally. Adjust seasoning with salt, pepper, and paprika to taste.
  8. Add the shrimp and sausage back into the skillet. Stir to combined then remove the heat and Enjoy!
  9. Top with chopped parsley and parmesan if desired. Serve with brown rice, cauli rice (low carb option), or steamed veggies

Tips

  • If sauce is too loose add a little cornstarch mixed with water (slurry) and simmer until thickened (repeat for desired consistency)
  • After adding sausage and shrimp back to the pan. be sure to take off the heat quickly so the shrimp won't over cook.
  • Adding the cream changes the flavor, adjust seasoning to taste. You can also add more Cajun seasoning and paprika for color and taste
  • Brown chicken sausages well for texture purposes

Spicy Chicken taquitos

Ingredients

    For spicy shredded chicken filling

  • 2 large chicken breast
  • 1 jar of salsa (spicy)
  • 1 tablespoon of jerk seasoning
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions

  1. place chicken in crock pot
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper
  3. pour salsa over chicken to cover
  4. add jerk seasoning
  5. set crock pot to high, cover, and cook for at least 4 hours
  6. shred with a fork and enjoy

Ingredients

    For spicy chicken taquitos

  • 2 cups shredded chicken
  • 4 ounces cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 1/2 cups shrap cheddar cheese (grated)
  • 4 ounces jalapenos or chile of choice
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 Tbs fajitas seasoning
  • 10 small flour tortillas
  • olive oil or cooking spray
  • Optional: sour cream, Gaucamole, pico de gallo, lime, hot sauce

Directions

  1. Prepare shredded chicken
  2. In a bowl combine and mix the chicken (drained of juice), softened cream cheese, chilies, and cheddar cheese set aside
  3. preheat air fryer to 400° for 3 minutes
  4. Lay a tortilla flat and place 3 tablespoons of the chicken cheese mix, close one end of the tortilla then roll tightly starting at the filled side. repeat with remaining tortillas
  5. Place fold side down in air fryer. Make sure they are not touching in basket. Lightly brush or spray taquitos with oil.
  6. Cook 6-8 minutes at 400° until crispy and golden. Be sure to flip them half way through.
  7. Serve hot, add toppings if desired and enjoy!

Salted S'mores Truffles

Ingredients

  • 3 cups graham cracker
  • 2 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups marshmallow fluff (might need more)
  • 2 cups milk chocolate chips for dipping
  • 1 tbs of coconut oil
  • Course salt for garnish

Directions

  1. Use food processor to crush up graham crackers into fine crumbs (you can also do this by hands)
  2. Place crumbs in large bowl add chocolate chips, marshmallows, and 1/2 tsp of salt
  3. mix until well combined
  4. Roll into balls then place them on a parchment lined sheet tray freeze for 16 min.
  5. Microwave chocolate chips and coconut oil in 30 sec intervals stirring in between until smooth and melted.
  6. Dip balls in chocolate then place them back on parchment and freeze for another 7 min until chocolate sets.s
  7. Remove, sprinkle salt, and enjoy!

Tips

  • Dpray hands and gloves with olive oil or coconut oil to avoid sticking.
  • Recipe yeilds 24 truffles
