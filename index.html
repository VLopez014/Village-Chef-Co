Meet The Chef

Hello,
My name is Imani and welcome to Village Chef & Co. I am a culinary school graduate; professionally cooking for 5+ years. Born and raised in the DMV area; this has exposed me to many cultures and cuisines. Giving me the freedom to explore culinary in many ways. Working in country clubs, corporate settings, and private restaurants sharpened my skills. However, one theme always stayed the same no matter where I went. Nothing makes me happier than for my food to bring you joy, smiles, and satisfaction. I consider good food a passion, As a result Village Chef & Co. was created. My team and I look forward to serving you soon.

- Chef Mani
